What Moisturizers Work Best for Dry Skin?

For dry skin, choose a moisturizer with hydrating ingredients like hyaluronic acid, glycerin, and ceramides. Richer creams with shea butter or oils provide deep nourishment. Fragrance-free options are best for sensitive skin. Occlusives help prevent moisture loss.

How to Get Glowing Skin Naturally?

Achieving naturally glowing skin requires a healthy skincare routine, including regular cleansing, gentle exfoliation, hydration, a diet rich in antioxidants, vitamins, and healthy fats, daily sunscreen, and adequate sleep, all combined to create a radiant glow.

The Best Sunscreens for Everyday Use

Choose sunscreens with SPF 30 or higher, mineral-based ingredients like zinc oxide or titanium dioxide, or chemical sunscreens with avobenzone or octinoxate for an invisible finish. Choose a sunscreen suited to your skin type for daily protection.
